Chocolate Cookies

Ingredient List:

Ingredient Amount
Sugar 2 cups
Margarine, softened 1 ¼
Large eggs 2
Vanilla 2 teaspoons
All-purpose flour 2 cups
Unsweetened cocoa powder ¾ cup
Baking sode 1 teaspoon
Salt ⅛ teaspoon
Chopped walnuts 1 cup

Instructions:

  1. Step: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Step: Cream sugar and margarine in a large bowl until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  3. Step: Combine flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt in a separate bowl. Add to the creamed mixture and mix until just blended. Stir in walnuts. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  4. Step: Bake in the preheated oven until edges are set and centers are soft, 8 to 10 minutes. Let sit briefly before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
